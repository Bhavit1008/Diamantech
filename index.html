About Us

Diamantech India Pvt. Ltd. manufactures and develops a comprehensive range of excellent stone cutting tools that makes the company the market leader. It commenced its journey in 2019 and now with time has expanded its boundaries globally. Diamantech's Managing Director Mr. Gajendra Chittora holds an expertise of 28 years in the market and Co-Director Mr. Kushagra Chittora upholds innovation that underpin the growth of the company and give the company a new edge in the market.

COMPANY'S VISION:

To use finest technologies that enable the production of cost efficient tools and serve our clientele with maximum value.

COMPANY'S MISSION:

To become the most reliable company in India that meets the future of stone cutting tools internationally.

Diamantech India is headquartered in Udaipur, Rajasthan and operates with a vision to deliver elite tools that render undisturbed and steady performance to help cut all types of stone across the globe. Its premium quality product portfolio encompass:

  • GANGSAW SEGMENTS
  • WIRESAW BEADS/CHAINS
  • SINGLE BLADE SEGMENTS
